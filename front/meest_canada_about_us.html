Welcome to Meest Canada - your reliable partner for international parcel and cargo delivery from Canada and the USA to Ukraine, Europe, Central Asia, and Israel, and beyond.

Meest was established in Toronto in 1989, and its our founder, Rostyslav Kisil aimed to unite the Ukrainian diaspora in Canada with their homeland by providing means to send parcels to support loved ones. Today, we are proud to have built bridges that connect people, organizations, and projects around the world.

WHY USE INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING SERVICE BY MEEST?

At Meest, we prioritize parcel delivery to Ukraine, but our services extend to 44 countries in Europe, Central Asia, the Caucasus, and Israel. In addition to parcel delivery, we offer money transfers to Ukraine, e-commerce solutions, and commercial cargo transportation.

HELP FROM MEEST CANADA

During this difficult time for Ukrainians, we are committed to providing affordable international delivery to Ukraine. We have been the main partner of sending humanitarian aid to Ukraine through well-known charities such as the Chernobyl Children's Fund, the Educational rehabilitation center "Dzherelo" in Lviv, and the Bridge of Hope, a joint charity project that provides care for orphans in Ukraine. We also provide humanitarian assistance to refugees who have been forced to leave their homes. Meest Canada in partnership with BCU Ukraine send 500 000 kg of humanitarian aid to Ukraine.
