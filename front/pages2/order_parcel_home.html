Order pick-up

You can save time and send more parcels from the comfort of your home!

We have a new service where our partner picks up your packagesfrom your home anywhere in Canada, and Meest ships them to your loved ones!

How to order home pick-up:

1

Register or log in to the Meest portal.

2

Register your package on the Meest portal. Choose HOME PICK-UP BY CANPAR, use your home address as a pick-up address, and select a working day for the courier to come.

3

Pay for your package on the Meest portal.

4

You will receive a barcode by email. Print it and stick it to your package.

5

The courier from our partner CANPAR will pick up your package the next working day during working hours or on the working day you specified.
