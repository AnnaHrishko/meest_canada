HOW DO I ORDER A COURIER HOME PICK-UP?

A courier from our partner CANPAR will come to you. The person will pick up your parcel the next working day during working hours, or on the working day you specified. The parcel will be delivered to the head office of Meest, and quickly sent to the recipient's address.

IMPORTANT The courier of our partner CANPAR will not communicate with you by phone or SMS before his visit.
